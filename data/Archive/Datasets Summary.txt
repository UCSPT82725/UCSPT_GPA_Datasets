URL List with Content Summary

1. GSS: https://gss.norc.org/us/en/gss/get-the-data.html
   • Thousands of survey responses capturing socioeconomic, education, and digital access data.
   • Suitable for predictive modeling of societal trends.

2. Tox21: https://tripod.nih.gov/pubdata
   • Dataset of 8,500 chemical compounds with toxicity profiles.
   • Useful for health risk models and compound safety predictions.

3. NOAA Paleoclimatology: https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/paleoclimatology
   • Historical temperature, CO₂, and emissions data spanning millennia.
   • Supports food security and climate risk forecasting.

4. 1000 Genomes Project: https://www.internationalgenome.org/data
   • Over 250 million human genetic variants from global populations.
   • Enables personalized medicine and genetic prediction models.

5. Earth BioGenome Project: https://www.earthbiogenome.org/
   • Genomic and ecological data from diverse eukaryotic species.
   • Essential for biodiversity tracking and food system modeling.
